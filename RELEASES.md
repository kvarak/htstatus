# Releases

**Purpose**: User-focused summaries of new features and improvements in HattrickPlanner
**Audience**: Hattrick managers who want to understand what's new without technical details
**Format**: Only versions with user-facing changes that you'll actually notice

*For detailed technical changes, see [CHANGELOG.md](CHANGELOG.md)*

---

## v3.8 - January 2026

- **New Community Feedback System**: Share suggestions and vote on features you want
- Submit ideas for improvements and see what other managers are requesting
- Vote on suggestions to help prioritize development

## v3.7 - January 2026

- **Formation Testing Tools**: Test different team formations and tactical strategies
- Experiment with player positions before making changes in Hattrick

## v3.5 - January 2026

- **Automatic Player Groups**: New users get seven sensible default groups created automatically
- No more manual setup - Goalkeepers, Defenders, Midfielders, Wingers, Forwards, Youth, Veterans ready to use

## v3.0 - January 2026

- **Fixed Login Issues**: Resolved problems with connecting to Hattrick and updating player data
- Much more reliable data synchronization with your Hattrick teams

## v2.0 - December 2025

- **Complete Interface Redesign**: Modern, faster, more responsive design
- Better mobile and tablet support for managing your team on the go
- Improved navigation and page layouts throughout the app

## v1.0 - February 2023

- **Initial Release**: First version of HattrickPlanner
- Connect your Hattrick account to sync and analyze player data
- View detailed player statistics and track team development
